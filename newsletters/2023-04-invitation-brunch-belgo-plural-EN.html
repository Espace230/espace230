About the Exhibition

Morphologie des plantes, a group exhibition in boreal colours, presents an interpretation of wild nature through a series of landscape and abstract works.

Plant morphology is a scientific term for the study of the shape, colour, development and structure of plants.

Through these works, the three artists (Kara Eckler, Luis Fernando Suárez and Louis-Bernard St-Jean) respectively present their own interpretation of nature, where crosses between abstraction and figuration lend themselves various games of textures, colours and shapes.
